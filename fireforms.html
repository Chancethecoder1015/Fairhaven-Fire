  • Smoke/Carbon Monoxide Detector Information
  • To schedule a smoke detector inspection,
  • Fill Out The Smoke/Heat Detector Form
  • Email the filled out form to admin@fairhavenfire.org
  • After you have emailed the form, call 508-994-1428 between the hours of 8AM-12N or 1PM - 4PM, Monday through Friday so we can schedule an inspection for you.
  • Or you can fill it out and bring it to fire department headquarters at 146 Washington Street during those same hours. If you have any questions regarding the form please call fire headquarters during normal business hours.
